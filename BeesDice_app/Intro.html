In this experiment, you will be reporting your financial and accounting income.
You will roll digital dice to determine the value of the incomes.
You need to report the total amount of your dice rolls.
The rolls are in a random order, but can be traced back to you using an audit/SEC check.
After rolling for your financial income, students selected to be part of the SEC have the option of verifying your income.
They have limited ability to check and most will not be.
Your accounting income will be a separate roll because of things like deferred taxes
When you report your accounting income, there is a 10% chance of getting audited.
If your financial income is not the same as the dice rolls during an SEC check, it may trigger an audit (10% chance)
If your accounting income is not the same as the dice rolls during an audit, the SEC will be informed and can choose to check
